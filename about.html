The Beginning

Sun & Wind Ceramics began not in a studio, but in a quiet moment of discovery.

Our name, Sun & Wind, is a tribute to the elements that inspire our work. The sun, for its warmth and light that reveals the true color of glazes, and the wind, for its untamed spirit, representing the unique and organic forms each piece takes. We're a small, independent studio dedicated to crafting ceramics that connect with the soul.
